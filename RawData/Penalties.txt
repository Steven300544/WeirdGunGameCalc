Attachment Penalties:
ARs: 30% less buffs from Sniper attachments, 20% less buffs from SMG attachments, 25% less buffs from LMG attachments
Snipers: 30% less buffs from Assault Rifle attachments, 40% less buffs from SMG attachments, 20% less buffs from LMG attachments
SMGs: 20% less buffs from Assault Rifle attachments, 40% less buffs from Sniper attachments, 35% less buffs from LMG attachments
LMGs: 25% less buffs from Assault Rifle attachments, 20% less buffs from Sniper attachments, 35% less buffs from SMG attachments
All debuff strength's stay at 100% regardless of attachment category
